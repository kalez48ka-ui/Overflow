The Problem

Passive Fandom

600M+ PSL viewers have zero financial upside from understanding the game

Betting Is Not Investing

Traditional betting is binary win/lose. No way to invest in a team's long-term performance

No On-Chain Sports

DeFi has tokens for everything -- except real-time sports performance. This market doesn't exist yet.

The Solution

Overflow -- A decentralized PSL team token exchange

Each PSL team has its own ERC-20 token. Prices are driven by a bonding curve -- as more fans buy in, the price rises algorithmically. When a team wins, holders earn performance rewards. When upsets happen, the Upset Vault pays out multipliers.

Token Mechanics

Battle-tested DeFi primitives adapted for sports

Asymmetric Bonding Curve

Buy: supply1.5 | Sell: supply1.2 -- rewards early believers

Progressive Sell Tax

12% under 5min, 8%, 5%, 3% after 2hrs -- discourages quick flips

Circuit Breaker

Auto-pauses trading on 15% drop in 5 minutes -- flash crash protection

Performance Rewards

Winning team holders share rewards -- ranked [30%, 22%, 17%...]

Upset Vault

When underdogs win, vault pays multiplied returns to early backers

Predict and Earn

Answer match questions, earn accuracy-based rewards -- 10/30/60 tier split
